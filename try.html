About

Cecilio Apostol Elementary School is a proud institution under the Department of Education - Schools Division Office of Caloocan. Located along 7th Avenue, Maria Clara Street, Caloocan City, our school is committed to nurturing young minds through quality and inclusive basic education.

Cecilio Apostol Elementary School is committed to providing quality basic education to all learners. We offer Kindergarten, Elementary (Grades 1-6), ALS, and SPED programs under the Schools Division Office of Caloocan.
